Zoolandia encompasses an entire ecosystem of collectibles and utility. From our original Apefrenz NFT collection to our virtual gathering platform Third Space, this project spans every corner of Web3 and we’re constantly exploring new areas for more!

Who knows what surprises we’ll uncover together in Zoolandia?
